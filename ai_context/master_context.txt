# CAREER VYAS - MASTER CONTEXT (15-DAY LAUNCH)
## Version: Sprint Edition

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
Career Vyas - Career guidance platform for Indian school students (Class 8-12)
**Goal**: Launch working website in 15 days with core features

## 2. CURRENT ASSETS
- **20+ Mentors** from IITs, NITs, Medical Colleges
- **Existing community**: WhatsApp groups with mentors guiding students
- **Designs**: Figma links provided
- **Live reference**: https://career-vyas-freelance.vercel.app

## 3. 15-DAY LAUNCH SCOPE (MVP)
### MUST HAVE (Launch with these):
✅ Home page (hero + value prop + CTA)
✅ Mentors page (showcase 5-7 active mentors)
✅ Community page (WhatsApp group join)
✅ Blog (2-3 posts to start)
✅ Email signup form
✅ Webinar registration (for Day 14 webinar)

## 4. TARGET USERS
- Indian school students (Class 8-12)
- Parents of these students
- Language: Simple, student-friendly English

## 5. 15-DAY SUCCESS METRICS
- **Website live**: Day 7
- **First 50 users**: Day 14
- **Webinar attendees**: 20+ (Day 14)
- **WhatsApp group members**: 50+ by Day 15
- **Blog posts**: 2-3 published
- **Budget**: Under ₹1000

## 6. DESIGN PRINCIPLES (15-Day)
- Mobile-first (students use phones)
- One clear CTA per page
- Trust elements (mentor photos, real names)
- Fast loading (<2 seconds)
- No complexity

## 7. TECH STACK (ALL FREE)
- **Frontend**: Next.js + Tailwind
- **Hosting**: Vercel (free)
- **Database**: Supabase (free)
- **Email**: Resend (free tier - 100 emails/day)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics + Vercel (free)
- **Domain**: ~₹800/year (optional)

## 8. BRAND BASICS
- Name: Career Vyas
- Tone: Like an elder brother/sister guiding you
