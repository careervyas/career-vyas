# CAREER VYAS - MASTER CONTEXT (15-DAY LAUNCH)

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
Career Vyas - Career guidance platform for Indian school students (Class 8-12)
**Goal**: Launch working website in 15 days with core features

- **Existing community**: WhatsApp groups with mentors guiding students
- **Designs**: Figma links provided
- **Live reference**: https://career-vyas-freelance.vercel.app

✅ Explore (Careers, Courses, Exams, Colleges sections)

- Parents of these students
- Language: Simple, student-friendly English

## 5. 15-DAY SUCCESS METRICS
- **Website live**: Day 7
- **First 50 users**: Day 14
- **Webinar attendees**: 20+ (Day 14)

## 6. DESIGN PRINCIPLES (15-Day)
- Mobile-first (students use phones)
- One clear CTA per page
- Trust elements (mentor photos, real names)
- Fast loading (<2 seconds)

- **Database**: Supabase
