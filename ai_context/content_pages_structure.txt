# CAREER VYAS - PAGE STRUCTURE

## EXPLORE SECTION

### /explore (Main Landing)
- Hero: "Explore Careers, Colleges, Exams & Courses"
- Categories grid: Careers | Colleges | Exams | Courses
- Featured content (most viewed)
- Search bar

### /careers (All Career Profiles)
- List of 100+ careers with icons
- Filter by stream (Science/Commerce/Arts)
- Search functionality
- Each card: Title, summary, salary range

### /careers/[slug] (Career Detail)
- Career title & summary
- Key info: Duration, Salary, Demand
- Related Courses section
- Required Exams section
- Top Colleges section
- Similar Careers section
- Mentor spotlight (mentors in this field)

### /courses (All Courses)
- List of 100+ courses
- Filter by career, duration, type
- Search

### /courses/[slug] (Course Detail)
- Course title & description
- Duration & eligibility
- Careers this leads to
- Exams required
- Colleges offering this
- Syllabus overview

### /exams (All Exams)
- List of 100+ exams
- Filter by conducting body, level
- Upcoming exam dates

### /exams/[slug] (Exam Detail)
- Exam name & full form
- Key dates
- Pattern & syllabus
- Eligibility
- Courses covered
- Colleges accepting
- Preparation resources

### /colleges (All Colleges)
- 150+ colleges
- Filter by state, type, ranking
- Search

### /colleges/[slug] (College Detail)
- College name & type
- Location & ranking
- Courses offered
- Exams accepted
- Admission criteria
- Fees overview
- Placement summary
- Facilities

## ADMIN SECTION

### /admin
- Dashboard with metrics
- Users management
- Content management (all content types)
- Analytics
- Bookings overview
- Settings
