# CAREER VYAS - PAGE STRUCTURE

## MAIN NAVIGATION
- Home
- Mentoring
- Explore
- Community & Blogs

## EXPLORE SECTION

### /explore (Main Landing)
- Categories grid: Careers | Courses | Exams | Colleges
- Search & Filter functionalities

### /careers/[slug] (Career Detail)
- Name of the career profile
- Overview of career profile (roles & responsibilities)
- Benefits of choosing this profile as a career
- Basic procedure to approach this job
- Eligibilities, skill requirements & working environment
- Entrance exam to follow the route
- Course description with their expenses (12th and Graduation)
- Top institutions to pursue the course
- Type of specialisation available under this job profile
- Pay-Scales as per the experience
- Promotions & upgradings
- Top recruiting agencies for this profile
- Demands & Future Scope
- Top inspiring personalities who have also followed this profile

### /courses/[slug] (Course Detail)
- What is the course?
- Overview/Highlights of this Course
- Difference between the courses (vs similar courses)
- Streams/Fields of the course
- Why should you study or opt for this course?
- Career opportunities after graduating this course
- Further/Higher studies after this course
- Entry-level salary packages & average salary packages
- Popular entrance exams to get admission
- List of top colleges in India related to this course

### /exams/[slug] (Exam Detail)
- Name of the Exam & About the exam
- Overview/Highlights (Particulars & Details table)
- Eligibility criteria for this exam
- Paper pattern & marking scheme
- Available seats & participating institutions
- Important events & their dates
- Exam Application process & Selection procedure

### /colleges/[slug] (College Detail)
- Name of the College & Logo
- College description with campus images
- Important Information (Location, Address, Map link, Campus size, How to reach)
- Courses offered by the institute (Streams, Entrance exams, Eligibility)
- Fee structure (Course-wise & year-wise)
- Student Hostel details (Fees, Facilities, Images)
- Campus facilities (Library, Canteen, Sports, Wifi, etc.)
- Extracurricular activity (Clubs, Fests)
- Placement statistics
